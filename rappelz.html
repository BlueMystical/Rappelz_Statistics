So.. you want to see some cool numbers for your Guild or perhaps just for curiosity? Then you are in the right place, but first there is something you should know:

As you may already know Rappelz is a really old game, and so are their servers and websites, wich we need to conect to in order to gather the data we want to see here. The problem is that back in those ancient times security protocols like HTTPS were merelly science-fiction and Modern browsers do not like the old ways, at all.
In resume, your browser is Blocking the connections to the Rappelz sites because they are considered 'Unsecure'. In order to fix this, you need to do as follows:

  • Click the 'Get The Data' button, it wont do anything, but now you can..
  • Find that little 'Shield' icon on the address bar and Click on it
  • In the pop-up window, click 'Load anyway' or 'Load unsafe script'

Then reload the page (if it doesn`t automatically) and Click the 'Get The Data' button again.

NOTE: In Firefox the Shield icon will be shown on the Left side.
